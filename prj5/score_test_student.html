Web Developer Job Trends

The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics forcasts that the availability of jobs in Full Stack Development will rise from 160,000 and is projected to grow 13 percent from 2018 to 2028, much faster than the average for all occupations. Demand will be driven by the growing popularity of mobile devices and ecommerce.

As of Dec 25, 2019, the average annual pay for a Full Stack Software Developer in the United States is $108,866 a year.

While ZipRecruiter is seeing annual salaries as high as $165,500 and as low as $50,000, the majority of Full Stack Software Developer salaries currently range between $85,500 (25th percentile) to $127,000 (75th percentile) across the United States.
